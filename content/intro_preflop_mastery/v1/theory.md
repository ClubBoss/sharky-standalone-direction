# The Gym
Sharky: Knowing Kung Fu is useless if you are slow. In poker, you must react instantly.

## What are [[Preflop Charts]]?
Cheat sheets that tell you exactly what to do with every hand in every position.

Pros: You stop guessing. You play mathematically perfect poker ([[GTO]]).

Cons: You can become a robot.

## Why Train?
At the table, you have 10 seconds. You do not have time to calculate. You need to look at 99 on the Button and feel the Raise.

Sharky: Your goal is to make standard decisions automatically, so you can save your brain power for the hard stuff.
