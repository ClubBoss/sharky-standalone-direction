# Two Deterministic Ways To Win A Pot

Every hand ends in one of two outcomes:

1. Everyone else folds.
- You win immediately.
- Your cards do not need to be revealed.

2. Showdown.
- Remaining players reveal cards.
- Best five-card hand wins.

Practical decision lens:
- Value: bet when worse hands can call.
- Bluff: bet when better hands can fold.

In this module, you will repeat one simple objective: choose the line that wins by fold pressure in a basic spot.
