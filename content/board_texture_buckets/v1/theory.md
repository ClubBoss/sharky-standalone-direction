## Board Texture Buckets

- Dry boards (like A72r) pull into ace-high or paired textures with limited connectability, so aggression often stays profitable.
- Semi-wet boards mix connectors and high cards, inviting situational bets and responsive defense.
- Wet boards (like 987ss) flood the surface with straight and flush combos, favoring cautious check-calling.

Match each bucket to the right aggression rhythm to keep the table's pulse predictable.
