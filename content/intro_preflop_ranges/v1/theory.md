# The Traffic Light System
Sharky: A hand like Ace-Jack is a monster on the Button, but garbage Under the Gun. Why? Because of [[Range]]s.

## What is a [[Range]]?
It is the set of all hands you are willing to play from a specific position.

## Size Matters: [[6-Max]] vs. [[Full Ring]]
Before you look at the lights, count the players.

[[6-Max]] (Online Standard): Action is fast. Ranges are wider.

[[Full Ring]] (Live / 9-Max): The first 3 positions are the danger zone.

Sharky's Rule: In a 9-player game, playing from the first 3 seats is like walking through a minefield. Fold almost everything.

## Early Position ([[UTG]]) = Red Light
You have many players left to act behind you.

Strategy: Super [[Tight]].

Play: Only Top 10% (AA, KK, QQ, AK).

Warning: In 9-max, [[UTG]] is even tighter than in 6-max.

## Middle Position = Yellow Light
Strategy: Cautious.

Play: Top 15-20% (Add JJ, TT, AQ).

## Late Position ([[BTN]]) = Green Light
You are the boss.

Strategy: [[Loose]] / Aggressive.

Play: Top 40-50% (Add [[Suited Connectors]] 78s, Small Pairs 22).

Goal: Steal the [[Blinds]].
