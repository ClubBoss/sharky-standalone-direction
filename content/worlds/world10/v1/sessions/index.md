# Sessions
World goal: choose a clear post-core track and apply one consistent adjustment at the table.

Completion shape:
- `w10.s01` sets the baseline track read.
- `w10.s02`..`w10.s09` pressure-test the track through cash, tournament, mixed, switch, consistency, and deepening reps.
- `w10.s10` closes the core ladder with one integrated synthesis check.
