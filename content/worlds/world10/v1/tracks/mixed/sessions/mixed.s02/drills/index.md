# Drills
- find_seat_anchor: lock the acting seat before reading the mixed spot
- find_role_anchor: confirm the hijack position anchor before acting
- tap_flop_left_anchor: mark the first shared board cue
- tap_turn_anchor: confirm the turn before the pressure shift
- tap_river_anchor: finalize the late-street board cue
- tap_hole_left_anchor: tie the mixed read to the left hole card
- check: keep the mixed line stable when pressure is unclear
- bet: fire when the mixed flop cues support value and fold pressure
