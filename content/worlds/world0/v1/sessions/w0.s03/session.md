# Session w0.s03

## Objective
Strengthen broad table recognition by tracking a farther seat target, the full three-button row, and the board as it reaches the river.

## Scenario
Each rep keeps the learner one step farther from the blind anchors.
The learner confirms seat S2,
re-anchors both hole cards,
and follows the board from the turn to the river while distinguishing fold from raise.
The goal is to keep the wider table picture coherent under simple action prompts.

## Decision
Choose the answer that best matches the visible seat,
card,
street,
or action button cue in the spot.

## Explanation
This session widens the learner's World 0 scan without adding strategy pressure.
The learner practices keeping the table layout, board order, and basic action buttons aligned before moving to denser checkpoints.
