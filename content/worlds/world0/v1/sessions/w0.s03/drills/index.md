# Drills
- find_seat_s2: tap the seat labeled S2 to keep the table layout stable away from the blind anchors.
- choose_raise: choose raise to identify the third basic action button.
- tap_turn: tap the turn card to continue the board in order.
- tap_hole_right: tap the right hole card to keep the hero hand fully identified.
- tap_hole_left_as: tap the ace of spades to re-anchor the left hero card.
- choose_fold: choose fold to confirm the first basic action button.
- tap_river: tap the river card to close the board sequence.
