# Drills
- find_seat_s1: tap the seat labeled S1 to extend the seat layout one step past the blind anchors.
- choose_call: choose call to match the middle action button in the row.
- tap_flop_mid: tap the middle flop card to confirm the first board street.
- tap_hole_left_as: tap the ace of spades to keep the left hole-card anchor visible.
- find_btn: tap the dealer button seat to complete the table layout.
- tap_hole_right: tap the right hole card to finish the two-card hero read.
- tap_turn: tap the turn card to continue the board in order.
