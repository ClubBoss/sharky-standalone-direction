# Drills
- find_sb: tap the small blind seat to anchor the left blind.
- choose_fold: choose fold to recognize the first basic action button.
- tap_flop_left: tap the flop to confirm the first community-card street.
- tap_hole_left: tap the left hole card to identify the learner hand.
- find_bb: tap the big blind seat to anchor the second blind.
- tap_hole_right_ks: tap the right hole card to finish the learner hand read.
- choose_call: choose call to recognize the second basic action button.
- tap_river: tap the river to confirm the last board street.
