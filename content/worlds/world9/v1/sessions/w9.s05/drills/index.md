# Drills
- find_seat_s2_position: tap seat S2 to anchor the player you may exploit from position.
- find_btn_position: tap the button to compare positional leverage around the table.
- tap_flop_position: tap the flop to start the position-specific exploit read.
- tap_turn_position: tap the turn to extend the exploit pressure sequence.
- tap_river_position: tap the river to finish the late exploit context.
- tap_hole_right_position: tap the right hole cards to keep the learner hand in view.
- choose_call_position_control: choose call when position supports a controlled exploit instead of extra pressure.
- choose_raise_position_pressure: choose raise when position makes the exploit pressure cleaner.
