# Session w9.s05

## Objective
Practice position-specific exploit adjustment in plain learner-facing terms.

## Scenario
Each rep compares the same player tendency from different position frames.
The learner decides whether the exploit should stay the same or change because the positional advantage changed the practical pressure available.

## Decision
Choose the exploit adjustment that best fits the tendency once position is taken into account.

## Explanation
Some exploits work better in position, while others become riskier out of position.
The goal is to recognize that position changes how cleanly a real-player adjustment can be applied, even when the opponent tendency stays the same.
