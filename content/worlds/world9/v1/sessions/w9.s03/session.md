# Session w9.s03

## Objective
Build the first World 9 checkpoint by adjusting against calling-station behavior in plain language.

## Scenario
Each rep shows an opponent who continues too often and folds too rarely.
The learner decides when pressure becomes less attractive and when cleaner value-oriented lines should take priority.

## Decision
Choose the more practical adjustment against the calling-station tendency:
reduce weak pressure or lean more on clearer value.

## Explanation
Calling-station adjustment is the natural counterpart to overfold punishment.
The goal is to recognize that some opponents remove bluff space and reward cleaner value-heavy discipline instead.
