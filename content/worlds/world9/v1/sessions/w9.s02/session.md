# Session w9.s02

## Objective
Practice punishing common overfold tendencies without widening into advanced exploit trees.

## Scenario
Each rep shows a player pool or opponent tendency that folds too often under pressure.
The learner decides when the overfold signal is strong enough to justify leaning more aggressively on the spot.

## Decision
Choose whether the visible overfold tendency justifies a stronger pressure line than the baseline would.

## Explanation
Overfold punishment is one of the clearest first exploit adjustments.
The goal is not solver-grade frequency tuning, only a practical learner-facing read that extra folds create room for more pressure.
