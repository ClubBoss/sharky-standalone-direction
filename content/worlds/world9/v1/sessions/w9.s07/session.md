# Session w9.s07

## Objective
Practice exploit-and-blocker context in a bounded learner-facing way.

## Scenario
Each rep shows a player tendency together with a blocker cue that may strengthen or weaken an exploit line.
The learner decides whether the blocker context makes the adjustment cleaner, thinner, or too fragile to trust.

## Decision
Choose whether the blocker context supports, weakens, or cancels the exploit idea.

## Explanation
Exploit opportunities do not live in a vacuum.
Blocker context matters because a player read can look attractive at first, then become less reliable once the card-removal story no longer supports the pressure line.
