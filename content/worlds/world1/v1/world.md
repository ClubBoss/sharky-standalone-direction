# World 1

## Overview
Cognitive shift: decisions are not isolated clicks; they come from position, action order, and simple preflop discipline.
You move from memorizing labels to choosing the next action with a clear reason.

## Goals
- Practice position awareness (in position vs out of position) in repeated reps.
- Practice starting-hand discipline so early decisions stay clean.
- Practice initiative awareness: who can pressure and who must respond.
- Practice consistent action selection in common beginner spots.

## Completion criteria
- You can explain your action in one short sentence tied to position and action flow.
- Common mistake we fix: overplaying weak starts because the hand "looks playable."
