# Session w1.s02

## Objective
Continue the World 1 learner-facing expansion by teaching blind and button basics in plain preflop terms.

## Scenario
Each rep compares simple blind and button spots and asks whether the hand should continue or release from that seat.
The goal is to feel that late position usually gives cleaner options,
while the blinds often demand more caution and simpler discipline.

## Decision
Choose the continue-or-release decision that best fits the seat in the spot.

## Explanation
This is the blind-and-button continuation for the next World 1 expansion block.
Button and blind decisions do not feel the same because position changes who acts later and who must defend earlier.
This session keeps the lesson compact:
recognize the seat first,
then make the simplest fitting preflop choice.
