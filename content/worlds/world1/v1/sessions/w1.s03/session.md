# Session w1.s03

## Objective
Close the next World 1 learner-facing expansion block by teaching action-order tracking when pressure reaches you before any deeper strategy appears.

## Scenario
Each rep shows a simple preflop action sequence and asks who has acted,
who still can act,
and what that means for your next choice.
The goal is to notice that your decision depends on action flow, not just on the cards you hold.

## Decision
Choose the action that best fits the current pressure and action order in the spot.

## Explanation
This is the mixed checkpoint for the next World 1 expansion block.
World 1 also teaches that pressure arrives through order.
When action reaches you after earlier players have entered,
the hand is no longer a clean first-in choice.
This checkpoint builds the habit of reading the action sequence before deciding how to respond.
