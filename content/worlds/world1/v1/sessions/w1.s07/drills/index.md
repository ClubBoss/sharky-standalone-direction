# Drills
- choose_button_raise_in_position_focus_v1: button focus rep where late position gives the cleanest first-in raise.
- choose_cutoff_call_in_position_pressure_v1: cutoff focus rep where position makes the continue comfortable enough for a clean call after an open.
- choose_button_fold_in_position_discipline_v1: button focus rep where position helps a good continue, but does not rescue a weak hand from folding.
