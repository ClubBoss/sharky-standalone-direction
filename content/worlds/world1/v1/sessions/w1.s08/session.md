# Session w1.s08

## Objective
Reinforce out-of-position preflop discipline as the second focused World 1 tail block.

## Scenario
Each rep puts the learner in a seat that must act without position comfort and asks for the cleanest response.
The goal is to make out-of-position discipline feel tighter and more repeatable when the seat makes loose continues harder to justify.

## Decision
Choose the action that best fits the clean out-of-position response in the spot.

## Explanation
This block keeps the focused tail honest by showing that position disadvantage should tighten decisions instead of inviting guesswork.
The learner should notice when acting first makes a hand easier to release or only barely clean enough to continue.
Without position comfort,
the honest response should become more restrained,
not more hopeful.
