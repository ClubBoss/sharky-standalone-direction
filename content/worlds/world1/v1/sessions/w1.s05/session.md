# Session w1.s05

## Objective
Reinforce start-quality discipline as the second learner-facing World 1 repetition block.

## Scenario
Each rep shows a beginner preflop start and asks whether the hand is still clean enough to continue or should be released before trouble starts.
The goal is to stop tempting but weak-looking hands from slipping past early discipline.
Equity is your chance to win the pot if all cards fall.

## Decision
Choose the action that best reflects clean preflop start-quality discipline in the spot.

## Explanation
This continuation block keeps World 1 honest by repeating the same clean-start standard under slightly different pressure.
Hands that only look playable often create later confusion,
so this session reinforces releasing weak starts before position and pressure become harder to manage.
