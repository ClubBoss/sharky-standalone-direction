# Drills
- bridge_review_dry_cheap_continue_v1: continue on a calm board when the price is cheap and future streets stay manageable.
- bridge_review_wet_expensive_release_v1: release when a wet board, poor price, and future-street risk all point against continuing.
- bridge_review_paired_fair_price_continue_v1: continue when the board stays paired and the price is fair with little future-street danger.
- bridge_review_connected_future_street_release_v1: release when connected pressure and remaining-street risk outweigh a weak improvement path.
