# Session w2.s02

## Objective
Build the next World 2 bridge by identifying who is in position from seat relationships and postflop action order.

## Scenario
Each rep shows a simple seat relationship such as button versus blinds or cutoff versus button.
You succeed by noticing who acts later after the flop and using that to name the in-position player.

## Decision
Read the seat relationship first, then decide who acts later and therefore has position.

## Explanation
World 2 next adds table-order reasoning. Learners stop treating seats as labels only and start using action order to explain why one player has the positional advantage.
