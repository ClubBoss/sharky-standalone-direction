# Session w2.s07

## Objective
Practice holding price, board, and remaining-street reasoning steady across a short two-step sequence.

## Scenario
The same small board-and-price situation survives across two linked prompts.
First read what the board and offered cost suggest, then carry that same practical reasoning forward as one more street of pressure appears.

## Decision
Do not reset your practical read between steps.
Carry forward the same board clues, cost, and future-street risk as the scenario advances.

## Explanation
This session keeps World 2 on continue-or-release logic across a short sequence. Learners practice holding the same board truth, price pressure, and remaining-street opportunity in mind instead of treating each prompt like a disconnected fresh start.
