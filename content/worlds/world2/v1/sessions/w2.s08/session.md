# Session w2.s08

## Objective
Carry one practical World 2 read across two linked questions without losing track of board texture, price pressure, or improvement paths.

## Scenario
Step 1 reads what the current board is doing and how much pressure it creates.
Step 2 stays on that same board and asks whether the remaining improvement path still justifies continuing at the offered cost.

## Decision
Do not treat board texture, price, and improvement paths as separate islands.
Use the same practical read to answer the second question cleanly.

## Explanation
World 2 stays on practical continue-or-release logic even across linked prompts. Learners practice holding the same board clues, cost pressure, and improvement chances in mind instead of resetting their reasoning every time a new question appears.
