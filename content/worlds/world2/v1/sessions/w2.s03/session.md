# Session w2.s03

## Objective
Build the next World 2 bridge by identifying who keeps initiative after the last aggressive action.

## Scenario
Each rep shows a simple pressure spot after one player raised and the other continued.
You succeed by noticing who made the last aggressive action and whether that player is more likely to keep the pressure on the next street.

OOP, or out of position, means acting before your opponent on later streets.

## Decision
Read the action first, identify the last aggressor, then decide who owns initiative in the current spot.

## Explanation
World 2 then adds pressure ownership. Learners stop using aggression labels loosely and start tying initiative to the actual betting action that created the current spot.
