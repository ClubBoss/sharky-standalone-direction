# Drills
- choose_hero_has_initiative_open_vs_call: identify hero as the player with initiative after hero raised and villain called.
- choose_villain_last_aggressor_open_vs_call: identify villain as the last aggressor after villain raised and hero called.
- choose_hero_more_likely_to_continue_pressure: identify hero as more likely to keep the pressure after hero made the last raise.
- choose_call_facing_bet: turn the initiative bridge into one compact facing-pressure action choice.
- choose_raise_oop_isolation: add one compact isolation response so the initiative bridge teaches a second action when pressure comes back.
- choose_fold_oop_pressure: complete the compact response ladder with one weak out-of-position fold response.
