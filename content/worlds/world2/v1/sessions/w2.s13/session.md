# Session w2.s13

## Objective
Use one draw-heavy flop to connect texture, outs, and the price of continuing.

## Scenario
The same authored flop stays in view while the learner first reads pressure, then counts improvement, then decides whether the price is good enough to continue.

## Decision
This is an intuition bridge, not a formula lesson. Read the board, count the clean improvement path, then decide whether a simple continue feels justified.

## Explanation
This chain turns draw strength into table-useful intuition: dynamic boards plus real outs can justify continuing when the price stays manageable.
