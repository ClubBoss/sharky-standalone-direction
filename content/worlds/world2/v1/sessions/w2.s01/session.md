# Session w2.s01

## Objective
Build the first World 2 bridge by reading who wins from visible hand and board truth instead of guessing from raw confidence.

## Scenario
Each rep shows a simple showdown spot with visible hero cards, villain cards, and board cards.
You succeed by comparing what is actually made on the table before using any later World 2 decision language.

## Decision
Read the board and both hands first, then identify who wins or whether the board plays.

## Explanation
World 2 starts by grounding the learner in visible outcome truth. Before price, pressure, or future-street planning matters, the learner must reliably read what the cards already say at showdown.
