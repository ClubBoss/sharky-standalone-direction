# Session w3.s08

## Objective
Continue the final World 3 tail by reinforcing facing-open continue or fold discipline in clear preflop spots.

## Scenario
You face an open before acting in each rep. This final World 3 block asks whether the hand should continue cleanly or simply fold instead of forcing a weak continue.

## Decision
Use the hand bucket and the opened-pot frame together, then choose the cleanest preflop continue or fold.

## Explanation
This is the facing-open continue-or-fold continuation for the final World 3 tail. World 3 discipline improves when the learner stops treating every playable-looking hand as a continue, so facing an open should still produce compact, disciplined preflop choices.
