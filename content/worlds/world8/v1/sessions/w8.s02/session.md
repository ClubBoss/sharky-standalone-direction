# Session w8.s02

## Objective
Practice risk premium in plain language before any deeper tournament math appears.

## Scenario
Each rep compares a cash-style continue with a tournament-style continue.
The learner decides when busting carries enough extra cost that a stronger reason is needed to continue.
EV, or expected value, is the average result of making the same decision many times.

## Decision
Choose whether the tournament spot needs extra caution because the risk premium is higher.

## Explanation
Risk premium is the first practical tournament adjustment after simple ICM buckets.
Sometimes the same chip-EV-looking continue is no longer attractive because busting carries a larger penalty than in a cash-style decision.
