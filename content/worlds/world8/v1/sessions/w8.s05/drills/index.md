# Drills
- find_bb_cover: tap the big blind to anchor the covering stack.
- find_sb_cover: tap the small blind to compare the covered stack.
- tap_flop_covering: tap the flop to hold the tournament context on board.
- tap_turn_covering: tap the turn to keep the pressure sequence in view.
- tap_river_covering: tap the river to finish the covering-versus-covered read.
- tap_hole_left_covering: tap the left hole cards to anchor the covering hand.
- choose_call_covering: choose call when the covering stack can continue without overcommitting.
- choose_fold_covered: choose fold when the covered stack cannot justify elimination risk.
