# Session w8.s05

## Objective
Practice covering-versus-covered reasoning in plain learner-facing tournament terms.

## Scenario
Each rep compares two tournament players with different stack relationships.
The learner decides how being the covering stack or the covered stack changes practical freedom to pressure or continue.

## Decision
Choose which player has more practical freedom because of the stack relationship and tournament pressure.

## Explanation
Covering versus covered is one of the first concrete ways tournament pressure changes decision comfort.
The goal is not advanced final-table policy, only a bounded read on why equal-looking chip spots feel different when one player risks elimination more heavily.
