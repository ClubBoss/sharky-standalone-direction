# Session w8.s08

## Objective
Practice blocker-aware tournament pressure in a bounded World 8 way.

## Scenario
Each rep shows a tournament spot where blocker context changes how comfortable pressure or continuing should feel.
The learner decides whether the blocker cue makes a pressure line cleaner or makes survival caution more sensible.

## Decision
Choose whether the blocker context supports cleaner pressure or argues for more caution under tournament pressure.

## Explanation
Blockers matter differently when tournament life is part of the risk.
The goal is not advanced solver logic, only a bounded read on when blocker context makes pressure more believable or continuing more dangerous.
