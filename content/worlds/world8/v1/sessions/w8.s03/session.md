# Session w8.s03

## Objective
Build the first World 8 checkpoint by comparing survival pressure against aggression pressure.

## Scenario
Each rep shows a tournament spot where one side wants to preserve life and the other may be able to pressure harder.
The learner decides whether the spot is driven more by survival caution or by aggression opportunity.

## Decision
Choose which side of the pressure tradeoff is more important in the spot:
survival or aggression.

## Explanation
This checkpoint turns ICM buckets and risk premium into a first practical tournament contrast.
The goal is not final-table precision, only a bounded read on when survival pressure should dominate and when aggression pressure can still carry the decision.
