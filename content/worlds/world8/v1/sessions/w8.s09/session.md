# Session w8.s09

## Objective
Practice late-street ICM closure when survival pressure still matters at the end of the hand.

## Scenario
Each rep shows a late-street tournament decision where the pot is already developed but elimination risk still changes the best practical closure.
The learner decides whether tournament pressure should favor a more cautious finish or still allow committed pressure.

## Decision
Choose the late-street closure that best respects both the pot situation and the tournament pressure.

## Explanation
Late-street closure in tournaments is not just a smaller version of chip-EV poker.
The goal is to recognize when survival pressure still changes the practical finish of a hand, even after earlier betting has already shaped the pot.
