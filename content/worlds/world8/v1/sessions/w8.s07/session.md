# Session w8.s07

## Objective
Practice medium-stack tournament navigation where neither pure survival nor pure urgency fully dominates.

## Scenario
Each rep shows a medium-stack tournament spot with room to maneuver but meaningful payout pressure still in the background.
The learner decides how to balance patience, pressure, and survival concerns without treating the spot like either a cash game or a short-stack emergency.

## Decision
Choose the medium-stack posture that best fits the spot:
measured pressure,
measured caution,
or balanced navigation.

## Explanation
Medium stacks often live in the hardest middle ground.
The goal is to recognize that tournament navigation here is about balancing flexibility with survival pressure, not defaulting to either extreme.
