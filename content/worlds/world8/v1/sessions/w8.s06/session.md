# Session w8.s06

## Objective
Build the first World 8 checkpoint for short-stack ICM pressure without turning the lesson into full push-fold chart memorization.

## Scenario
Each rep shows a short-stack tournament spot where survival pressure and limited maneuverability both matter.
The learner decides when the short stack is forced toward urgency and when extra caution still matters.

## Decision
Choose whether short-stack pressure should make the spot more urgent, more cautious, or still balanced.

## Explanation
Short stacks feel tournament pressure more sharply because there is less room to wait for a perfect future spot.
The goal is not exact jam charts, only a bounded read on how reduced depth and survival pressure reshape the decision.
