# Session w7.s01

## Objective
Build the first World 7 bridge by sorting spots into simple stack-depth buckets.

## Scenario
Each rep shows an effective stack and asks whether the spot should be read as shallow,
medium,
or deep.
The goal is to notice that the same hand and board do not carry the same plans at every depth.

## Decision
Choose the stack-depth bucket that best fits the spot.

## Explanation
World 7 starts when learners stop treating stack size as background information.
Depth buckets make stack pressure concrete enough to see when room is limited, when flexibility opens up, and when future-street leverage starts to matter.
