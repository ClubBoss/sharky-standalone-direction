# Session w7.s03

## Objective
Introduce medium-stack flexibility as the balanced middle of World 7.

## Scenario
Each rep shows a medium-depth spot and asks which plan still keeps useful pressure and maneuvering room without pretending the stack is fully deep.
The goal is to notice that medium stacks support more options than shallow play while still demanding discipline.

## Decision
Choose the plan that best fits medium-stack flexibility in the spot.

## Explanation
Medium stacks sit between forced simplicity and deep leverage.
This is where learners start to see that pressure, caution, and optionality can coexist, so the correct read depends on how much room the stack still leaves for future action.
