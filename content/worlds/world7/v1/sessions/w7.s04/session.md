# Session w7.s04

## Objective
Teach deep-stack leverage as a practical World 7 skill.

## Scenario
Each rep shows a deeper-stack spot and asks which plan best uses the extra room that depth creates.
The goal is to notice that deep stacks reopen pressure,
delay,
and future-street leverage in ways that do not exist when stacks are short.

## Decision
Choose the plan that best reflects deep-stack leverage in the spot.

## Explanation
Deep stacks change the meaning of a decision because more money and more streets remain behind.
That extra room can create stronger pressure,
more maneuvering,
and more punishment for weak planning.
This session keeps the lesson bounded to leverage awareness rather than solver-heavy deep-stack lines.
