# Session w7.s02

## Objective
Teach shallow-stack discipline in plain learner-facing terms.

## Scenario
Each rep shows a shallow-stack spot and asks whether the situation calls for a tight continue,
a clear commitment,
or a simple release.
The goal is to feel how reduced depth removes comfortable maneuvering room.

## Decision
Choose the option that best reflects shallow-stack discipline in the spot.

## Explanation
Shallow stacks compress decisions.
Hands that might continue comfortably at deeper depths lose flexibility when there is not enough room to probe, delay, or recover from mistakes.
This session keeps the lesson practical: shallow depth rewards cleaner commitment and cleaner release.
