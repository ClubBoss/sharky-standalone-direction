# Session w7.s05

## Objective
Teach how stack depth can shift the correct plan once the hand reaches the turn.

## Scenario
Each rep shows a turn spot where the same line can feel very different depending on how much depth remains.
The goal is to notice that the turn is often where stack size starts to narrow one plan and strengthen another.

## Decision
Choose the turn plan that best fits the remaining depth in the spot.

## Explanation
Turn decisions are not only about the board.
They are also about how much stack remains to support pressure,
protection,
or controlled continuation on later streets.
This session teaches depth-shift reasoning on the turn without widening into river closure or blocker-specialist themes.
