# Drills
- find_sb_shift_turn: anchor the small blind before comparing how the turn depth shift changes the plan.
- find_bb_shift_turn: anchor the big blind so the turn depth comparison stays seat-true.
- tap_flop_reference_shift: revisit the flop card that set up the earlier depth context.
- tap_turn_depth_shift: lock the turn card where the remaining depth starts to change the plan.
- tap_river_depth_shift: close the depth-shift sequence by checking the final street context.
- tap_hole_right_shift: keep blocker and hand reference stable with the right hole card before the final response.
- choose_call_after_shift: call when the remaining depth still supports controlled continuation after the shift.
- choose_fold_after_shift: fold when the depth shift removes the pressure edge and the plan can no longer continue cleanly.
