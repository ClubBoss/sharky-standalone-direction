# Session w6.s02

## Objective
Practice reading whether a likely range is wider or narrower once position and line are considered.

## Scenario
Each rep compares two simple spots and asks which side arrives with more possible hands.
The learner uses position and action context to decide whether the range should feel wide or tight.

## Decision
Choose which range is wider or narrower before worrying about any exact hand.

## Explanation
Range width is the first practical step after bucket thinking.
Some positions and lines keep many hands alive, while others narrow quickly, and that changes who can pressure profitably even before deeper range math appears.
