- find_bb_river_polar: anchor the big blind before comparing final river range endings.
- find_sb_river_polar: anchor the small blind so the river pressure line stays seat-true.
- tap_flop_river_polar: revisit the flop card that started the range-shape story.
- tap_turn_reference: use the turn card as the compression reference before the river decision.
- tap_river_polar: lock the final river card before reading the ending as polarized or merged.
- tap_hole_right_polar: keep blocker reference stable with the right hole card before the final choice.

- choose_raise_polar: polarized endings raise when the range picture supports clear river pressure.
