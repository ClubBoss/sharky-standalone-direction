# Session w6.s10

## Objective
Range Synthesis Checkpoint: Integrate position, texture, blockers, and range density into one bounded reading-first range picture.

## Scenario
Each rep revisits a compact range spot and asks the learner to combine the major World 6 ideas:
range buckets,
range width,
flop advantage,
street compression,
blocker context,
and range transition by street.

## Decision
Synthesize the range signals into the clearest overall range read before choosing any simple next action.

## Explanation
This checkpoint is the bounded synthesis of World 6.
The goal is not deterministic solver-style resolution, only a stable learner-facing habit of reading the full range picture before falling back on a single-signal judgment.
