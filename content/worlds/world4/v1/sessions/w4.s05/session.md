## Objective
Repeat protection-intent sizing until charging vulnerable equity feels automatic.

## Scenario
These reps focus on opponents who want a cheap continue.
You are choosing whether protection should lightly tax the continue, charge it at the standard rate, or make it expensive immediately.

## Decision
Choose the size that best matches protection intent in each spot.

## Explanation
Protection sizing should feel distinct from pure value and pure bluff pressure.
This cluster stabilizes the practical ladder:
lighter lines still charge vulnerable equity,
standard lines make the continue uncomfortable,
and maximum lines make continuing expensive right now.
