# Session w4.s08

## Objective
Reinforce protection-intent sizing through cost and pressure checks.

## Scenario
These reps focus on when protection should be lighter, firmer, or fully punishing.
The learner chooses the preset that best charges vulnerable equity without confusing protection with pure bluff pressure.

## Decision
Choose the preset that best matches the intended protection cost.

## Explanation
Protection sizing should scale with how urgently the continue needs to be taxed.
This cluster keeps the cost judgment stable across repeated reps:
lighter protection still taxes realization,
firmer protection makes the continue meaningfully harder,
and the strongest line makes calling expensive right away.
