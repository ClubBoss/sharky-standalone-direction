# Session w4.s03

## Objective
Blend early World 4 purpose families through simple mixed purpose-and-price reads.

## Scenario
Each rep mixes value,
price-keeping,
bluff pressure,
and controlled reopen spots.
The learner compares practical price tradeoffs instead of treating every preset as interchangeable.

## Decision
Choose the size that best matches the clearest purpose-and-price tradeoff in the spot.

## Explanation
This checkpoint verifies that early purpose categories stay separate once the reps are mixed together.
The goal is not perfect sizing optimization,
only a stable habit of asking what the bet is trying to accomplish and what price that purpose creates.
