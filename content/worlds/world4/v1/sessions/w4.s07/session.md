## Objective
Reinforce value-intent sizing through price-aware follow-through reps.

## Scenario
These reps stay inside value but vary how strongly you want to charge.
The task is to keep the value goal clear while choosing a size that still fits the practical price you want the opponent to face.

## Decision
Choose the preset that best preserves value intent and the intended price effect.

Value follow-through is not just "bet because strong."
