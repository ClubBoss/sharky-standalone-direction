# Session w4.s09

## Objective
Reinforce bluff-intent sizing through pressure-versus-price tradeoffs.

## Scenario
These reps stay inside bluff intent while changing how much immediate fold pressure you need.
The learner compares smaller probing sizes, stronger pressure sizes, and controlled reopens without losing the bluff purpose.

## Decision
Choose the preset that best fits the bluff pressure tradeoff in the rep.

## Explanation
Good bluff sizing is not always maximum force.
This cluster trains when to probe, when to press harder, and when to reopen without bloating the line unnecessarily.
