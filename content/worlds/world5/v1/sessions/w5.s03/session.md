# Session w5.s03

## Objective
Extend board-state reading to wetter textures by recognizing connected boards and noticing how stronger draw or improvement pressure changes what matters next.

## Scenario
You stay in compact board-reading spots. Each rep asks you to examine the board first, identify whether the texture is more connected or unconnected, and connect that read to the most important draw or improvement cue before any action becomes relevant.

## Decision
Read the board state first: identify the wetter texture, then notice which draw paths or improvement pressures are now active.

## Explanation
Wet-board discipline starts with reading connectivity and improvement pressure correctly, not with anchor-first action flow. World 5 is still building texture, draw, and improvement awareness before deeper equity, pot-odds, and specialization layers appear.
