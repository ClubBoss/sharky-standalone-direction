# Session w5.s06

## Objective
Train in-position board reading by noticing how texture, draw pressure, and improvement cues shape the next reasonable response.

## Scenario
You stay in compact board-reading spots from in position. Each rep asks you to identify the most important texture, draw, or improvement cue on the current board first, then use that read to guide the simplest fitting response.

## Decision
Read the board state first: notice what the texture allows, what draws are live, and what improvement cues matter before choosing the simplest in-position response.

## Explanation
In-position play stays clearer when the board is read before the action is chosen. World 5 still focuses on texture, draws, and improvement cues, with action treated as downstream of reading the board correctly before deeper equity, pot-odds, or specialization layers appear.
