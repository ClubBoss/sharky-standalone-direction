# Session w5.s01

## Objective
Build the first board-state reading habit by recognizing simple texture buckets and noticing how draw or improvement potential changes what matters next.

## Scenario
You start with compact board-reading spots. Each rep asks you to look at the board first, label the texture in plain language, and connect that texture to the most important immediate draw or improvement cue before deeper action judgment appears.

## Decision
Read the board state first: identify whether the texture is dry or wet, then notice whether draws or clear improvement paths are present.

## Explanation
World 5 begins by shifting attention away from private-hand-first thinking. Texture, draws, and improvement potential now lead the reading process. This is still foundation work before deeper equity, pot-odds, and specialization layers arrive.
