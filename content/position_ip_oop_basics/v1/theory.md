## Position Basics - IP vs OOP

- Heads-up, you either act last (in position) or first (out of position) on every street.
- The button sits in position after the flop, closes action, and makes more informed decisions.
- The big blind is out of position; he must act before you, so he plays reactively until you finish.

Practice recognizing who controls the street and how that changes the pressure long before the river.
