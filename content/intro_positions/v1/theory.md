# Location, Location, Location
Sharky: In real estate and Poker, only one thing matters: where you sit.

## The [[Dealer Button]] ([[BTN]])
This is the best seat.

Why: You act LAST on every betting round ([[Flop]], [[Turn]], [[River]]).

Power: You get to see what everyone else does before you make a decision. Information is power.

## The [[Blinds]] ([[SB]] & [[BB]])
The worst seats.

Why: You act FIRST after the flop. You are playing blind.

Advice: Play very few hands here. Defensive mode.

## The Hierarchy
Early Position ([[UTG]]): Under the Gun. First to act preflop. Very dangerous. Needs strong cards.

Middle Position ([[MP]]): Average danger.

Late Position ([[CO]], [[BTN]]): The hunting ground. You can play many weak hands because you have [[Position]].

Sharky: I would rather play 7-2 on the Button than A-J in the Blinds. [[Position]] beats cards.
