# Checkpoint

## What You Should Know
- Seat position narrows openings early and widens late, with SB/BB using tailored lift/fold plans.
- Initiative allows compact c-bets on dry boards and confident pressure on turns, while absence demands defense.
- Showdown order rewards the bettor when a river bet is called; if the river checks through the first active left of the button shows first.
