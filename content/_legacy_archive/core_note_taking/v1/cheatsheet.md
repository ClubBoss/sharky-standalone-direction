# Cheatsheet

## Key Rules
- Keep notes atomic: record action, texture, size, and result in one short line.
- Tag players with stable traits (overfold_flop, calls_down, tiny_3bet) for reusable reads.
- Capture minimally during play and analyze deeply later to avoid missing action.
- Always note position and size family before routing each hand to fix_now/study_spot/player_read.

## Fast Heuristics
- Use short tags instead of sentences to keep capture fast and searchable.
- Route high-leverage hands to study_spot, misplayed ones to fix_now, and opponent tendencies to player_read.

## Quick Numbers
- Capture times: 1–2 seconds per hand to stay present.

## Examples (1–2 lines)
- "CO vs UTG SRP, K72r, call small, turn 5, fold to second barrel" routed to study_spot with a tag "folds_turn_vs_double_small."
