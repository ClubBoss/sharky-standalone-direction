What it is
Position-by-position guides for 9-max MTT final tables. Payout jumps create ICM pressure. Chip leader applies pressure, mediums pay highest risk premium, shorts use shove/call-off trees.


Why it matters
Chips do not equal cash. Calling off too light when covered can cost pay jumps. CL widens opens, 3-bets, and barrels. Playbooks clarify who to attack, who to avoid, and which sizes build fold equity without risking bust.

Rules of thumb
- Widen when you cover; tighten when covered. Shorts jam/fold mapped to 3bet_ip_9bb/3bet_oop_12bb; mediums avoid dominated flats OOP; CL exploits position/blockers.
- Preflop: 3bet_ip_9bb vs CO/BTN steals, 3bet_oop_12bb from blinds; 4bet_ip_21bb/4bet_oop_24bb value-lean. Bluff 4-bets rare unless you cover/top blockers.
- EP/MP stay disciplined; CO/BTN/SB expand only when covering. Squeeze (3bet_oop_12bb/3bet_ip_9bb) when you cover; avoid cold-calling OOP.
- Postflop: small_cbet_33 on safe static boards. half_pot_50 for clean commit lines with value+equity. big_bet_75 for size_up_wet/nut-advantage as cover. OOP, protect_check_range on middling.
- As cover, double_barrel_good on range turns; triple_barrel_scare only with blockers/story. Tag overfold_exploit vs covered mediums who fold turns.
- Plan flop+turn to avoid marginal river call-offs when covered. Prefer fold over thin peels that create ugly rivers.

Mini example
CO 40bb, BTN (CL) 65bb covers, blinds 25bb/30bb. CO opens 2.2bb. BTN 3bet_ip_9bb leveraging coverage. CO calls; pot ~20bb, stacks ~31bb, SPR ~1.6. Flop K72r: BTN small_cbet_33 pressures underpairs/queen-highs. Turn Qx: half_pot_50 as double_barrel_good, commit with Kx+/strong draws. River: BTN triple_barrel_scare only with blockers; else check back. If CO covers BTN, BTN defends tighter/reduces turn barrels.

Common mistakes
- Calling 3-bets/4-bets too light when covered (ICM makes calls expensive).
- Flatting OOP as medium stack, inviting squeezes/bad SPRs.
- Using big_bet_75 without size_up_wet equity/nut share; get check-raised/over-commit as covered player.
- Bluff 4-betting without coverage/blockers; 4bet_ip_21bb/4bet_oop_24bb should be value-lean.
- Ignoring overfold_exploit on turns vs capped mediums; leaving EV on table as cover.

Mini-glossary
Coverage: who can bust whom; being covered increases your risk premium.
Risk premium: extra equity needed to continue because busting is costly.
Commit line: pre-planned two-street sequence to all-in with value+equity.
Position playbook: default actions by position under ICM and coverage.
EV: Expected Value - the average amount you'd win or lose if you made the same play many times

Contrast
Compared to non-ICM middle stages, FT playbooks prioritize coverage-first pressure and fold equity over thin chip-EV calls. Versus PKO FTs, you ignore bounty overlay here; trees are tighter, 4-bets value-lean, and postflop aggression is gated by ICM rather than bounty incentives.

_This module uses the fixed families and sizes: size_down_dry, size_up_wet; small_cbet_33, half_pot_50, big_bet_75._
