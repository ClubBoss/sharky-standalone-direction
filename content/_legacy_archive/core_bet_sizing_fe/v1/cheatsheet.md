# Cheatsheet

## Key Rules
- Small bets (25-33%) on static boards keep merge ranges alive and charge floats.
- Medium bets (40-60%) work when you plan multiple streets on mixed textures.
- Large bets (66-100%) suit dynamic textures or high-equity value/bluff combos.
- Overbets (125-200%) belong to polar lines when opponents are capped.
- Pair polar ranges with large/overbet sizes and merge ranges with small/medium; mismatch leaks value.

## Fast Heuristics
- K72r: small bet to keep pressure.
- Dynamic turn/river: shift to large or even overbets when equity or blockers align.
- Always align frequency with size families (size_down_dry, size_up_wet, small_cbet_33, half_pot_50, big_bet_75).

## Quick Numbers
- Small ≈ 25-33% pot, medium ≈ 40-60%, large ≈ 66-100%, overbet ≈ 125-200%.

## Examples (1–2 lines)
- UTG uses small on K72r, medium on a draw-heavy turn, and overbet on a blank river with polar combos.
