- Flop J95r checks around. Turn A adds IP [[term:RANGE_ADVANTAGE]] strength, so BTN delay_turn half_pot_50; BB folds, MP calls. River brick: BTN fires small for thin [[term:VALUE]]. When the turn pairs 9, checks go through and BB [[term:PROBE]]_turns half_pot_50 to punish passive ranges.
