# Recap

## Key Ideas
- Mental routines (pre-session checklist, stop-loss, time box, A-game cue) keep decisions clear through swings.
- Fast tilt interrupts and variance literacy protect attention and bankroll when emotions spike.

## Common Mistakes
- Chasing losses with longer sessions, which multiplies fatigue and emotional errors.
- Reviewing hands mid-session, which drains focus and derails execution.

## Quick Checks
- What does your pre-session checklist include?
- How do you interrupt tilt without breaking focus?

## Mini-Exercise (1–2 lines)
- After a bad stretch, describe how you use a breathe-reset, table cut, and a short walk to stay calm.
