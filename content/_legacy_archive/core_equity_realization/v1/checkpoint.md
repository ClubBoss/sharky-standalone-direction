# Checkpoint

## What You Should Know
- Position, initiative, SPR, texture, and player count govern equity realization; heads-up and low SPR situations realize more.
- Keep SPR manageable through the turn so your river plans stay credible and you can extract thin value.
- Multiway pots demand tighter calls and disciplined raises because extra players sap realization.

## Self-Assessment
- I can explain how position and initiative turn raw equity into chips.
- I monitor SPR and avoid bloated rivers that kill value or bluff plans.

## Quick Scenario (1–2 lines)
- You have initiative on a dry turn after a small flop bet; describe how you size to keep SPR low and what you expect on the river.

## Reflection
- Let texture and initiative dictate your sizing choices so that equity realization stays predictable instead of reactive.
