# Cheatsheet

## Key Rules
- Use position and initiative to realize equity: the last aggressor can bet, check, or take free cards to preserve edge.
- Keep SPR low with medium-strength hands so you can value bet without folding under pressure.
- Match texture and sizing: small c-bets on dry boards, bigger bets on wet ones, and avoid multiway bloats that reduce fold equity.

## Fast Heuristics
- Dry boards + initiative → small bets and tight SPR to keep the river simple.
- Wet boards or multiway spots → lean toward larger bets to deny equity and rely on realization heads-up.
- Manage turn/river sizings so SPR stays under 1 when you plan a river shove.

## Quick Numbers
- Target SPR <1 on the river when stacking for a jam-friendly finish.
- Heads-up realization exceeds multiway because each extra player drains fold equity.

## Examples (1–2 lines)
- UTG with initiative and a dry board keeps SPR low by betting small on the turn and forcing folds on the river.
