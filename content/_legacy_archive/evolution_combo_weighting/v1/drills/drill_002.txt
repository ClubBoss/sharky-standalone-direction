# What to do
Slot each combo group into value, bluff, protection, or merge weightings for a mixed cluster with uneven equity and blockers.

# Input
Cluster: strong top pairs, mid suited connectors, weak offsuit broadways, plus blocker-heavy combos that deny high cards.

# Expected reasoning
Top pairs anchor value at full weight.
Blocker-heavy combos work as partial bluffs because they reduce opponent nuts.
Mid suited connectors require protection weight to shield fragile equity.
Weak offsuit broadways fill merge or thin value roles depending on density pressure.

# Correct answer
Value: top pairs full; bluff: blocker combos partial; protection: mid connectors medium; merge: weak offsuit broadways light.

# Common mistakes
- Weighting every bluff the same instead of favoring blockers.
- Shifting mid connectors into merge before protection.
- Flattening merge combos into value without checking density.
- Starving protection to feed marginal bluffs.
