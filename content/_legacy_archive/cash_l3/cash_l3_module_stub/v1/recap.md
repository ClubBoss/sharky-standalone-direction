# Cash L3 — Recap

## Key Ideas
- Size and range choices must track effective stack depth.
- Preserve fold equity versus stations; avoid bloating pots without value.
- Thin value is fine when ranges stay wide; tighten when ranges cap.

## Practical Reminders
- BTN vs BB single-raised pots: small c-bets keep weak pairs indifferent.
- 3-bet pots: call with strong pairs in position, avoid dominated offsuit hands.
- Short stacks: price-aware players call wider; bluff less, value tighter.

## Fast Examples
- BTN opens 2.2bb, BB calls, flop K72r: small bet Kx, check back weak pairs.
- CO faces BTN 3b to 8bb with JJ: call, play turns straightforward.

## Final Checklist
- Know stack bands (20/40/80bb) and adjust opens and 3-bets accordingly.
- Pressure capped ranges; do not over-bluff sticky players.
- Enter pots with hands that realize equity; fold weak offsuit broadways early.
