# Theory Micro-Loop


## Key Idea
- Multiway pots share [[term:EQUITY]] across players, so position, tight calling, and conservative sizings protect realization while the aggressor still extracts value.

## Mini-Example
- UTG opens 2.5bb, CO and BTN call. Flop K72r: UTG small_cbet_33, CO folds, BTN calls. Turn 5x: UTG half_pot_50 to [[term:DENY_EQUITY]], BTN folds. The small dry flop size, cautious turn bet, and respect for call/back fold options keep the plan efficient.

## Actionable Rules
- Use size_down_dry on Axx/Kxx dry boards; small_cbet_33 denies [[term:EQUITY]] and forces broadways to pay.
- Size_up_wet only with strong [[term:EQUITY]] (overpairs, strong top pairs, combo draws); otherwise check and protect_check_range.
- Bias squeeze_iso_3bet toward value with [[term:BLOCKERS]]/playability; avoid light squeezes OOP to dodge avoid_bloated_pot_oop spots.
- Treat raises as strong: continue with top pairs, overpairs, and strong draws, and raise_for_protection on volatile textures; fold marginal pairs and gutters.
- On the turn, [[term:DENY_EQUITY]]_turn when scary cards appear; use delay_cbet_ip/oop when the flop favored you.
- On the river, polarize_river when ranges cap and draws miss; take value_thin_ip after checks with kicker advantage, and fold more OOP without clarity.
