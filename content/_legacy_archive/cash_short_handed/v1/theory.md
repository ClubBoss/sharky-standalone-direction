What it is
Short-handed means playing 4-5 handed most of the session and sometimes 3-handed on 6-max tables. With fewer players, ranges widen, position changes value faster, and the blinds come around more often. You open first-in more, defend blinds more, and face more heads-up pots while keeping the same size families but higher action [[term:FREQUENCY]].


Why it matters
More steals and defenses create many marginal nodes. 3-bets lower [[term:SPR]] so decisions simplify and [[term:BLOCKERS]] matter more. Population [[term:LEAK]]s amplify: under-defended blinds, over-cbet on low boards, and [[term:OVERFOLD]]s to OOP turn barrels. Disciplined, size-led plans convert these into [[term:EV]].

Rules of thumb
- First-in opens: widen 4-5 handed, but tighten if a hyper-aggressive reg sits on your left. Prefer BTN/SB steals; trim weakest offsuit CO opens under pressure.
- 3-bet families: 3bet_ip_9bb in position and 3bet_oop_12bb out of position; add value + [[term:BLOCKERS]] bluffs versus BTN/SB steals, stay value-lean versus earlier seats.
- 4-bet families: 4bet_ip_21bb and 4bet_oop_24bb keep leverage and tidy [[term:SPR]]; deploy more versus frequent 3-bettors and bluff less versus tight ranges.
- Flop sizes: small_cbet_33 on dry Axx/Kxx; half_pot_50 on middling; big_bet_75 only when size_up_wet is justified by strong [[term:EQUITY]] or nut advantage.
- Lines and geometry: after a 3-bet, [[term:SPR]] ~3-5; favor initiative and position. Default small_cbet_33 IP and half_pot_50 OOP unless size_up_wet; protect_check_range OOP on middling; delay_turn and [[term:PROBE]]_turns when the turn favors you.
- SB strategy: raise-first-in is preferred; complete only with clear multiway incentives. Tighten completes if BTN is sticky or 3-bets often. 

Mini example
CO opens 2.3bb, BTN 3-bets to 9bb (3bet_ip_9bb); blinds fold. Flop K72r: BTN small_cbet_33 ~6.5bb; CO calls. Turn 5x: BTN half_pot_50 to deny versus 7x/pp/gutters and to set river geometry. On T98ss, size_up_wet with big_bet_75 only with strong [[term:EQUITY]]; otherwise protect_check_range.

Common mistakes
- Copying full-ring opens 4-5 handed. Why it is a mistake: leaves [[term:EV]] on the table or opens into bad seats. Why it happens: habit and fear of 3-bets.
- Auto small_cbet_33 on dynamic boards then calling raises without a plan. Why it is a mistake: grants cheap realization then pays off polar lines. Why it happens: overgeneralizing dry-board success.
- Barreling river without triple_barrel_scare conditions. Why it is a mistake: burns chips into sticky bluff-catchers. Why it happens: commitment bias after turn barrel.

Mini-glossary
3bet_ip_9bb / 3bet_oop_12bb: short-handed 3-bet size families by position to keep leverage and [[term:SPR]] in line.
4bet_ip_21bb / 4bet_oop_24bb: standard 4-bet ladders that set low [[term:SPR]] and preserve fold [[term:EQUITY]].
small_cbet_33 / half_pot_50 / big_bet_75: core sizing families mapped to dry, middling, and dynamic textures.
protect_check_range / delay_turn / [[term:PROBE]]_turns / double_barrel_good / triple_barrel_scare: tools to realize [[term:EQUITY]] and apply pressure at the right moments.
[[term:EV]]: Expected Value - the average amount you'd win or lose if you made the same play many times

Contrast
Full-ring instincts fail here; short-handed rewards wider first-in, disciplined 3-bet/4-bet ladders, and texture-driven small_cbet_33 with timely delay_turn and probe_turns.
