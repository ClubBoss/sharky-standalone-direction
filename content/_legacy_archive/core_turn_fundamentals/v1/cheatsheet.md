# Cheatsheet

## Key Rules
- Barrel when the turn improves your range or hurts theirs; fold or check when it ties the caller into stronger hands.
- Size by purpose: use bet_big_turn on dynamic turn cards, bet_small_turn to deny equity when value is thin.
- Bluff more with blockers that hurt top pairs/draws and reduce bluffing when you unblock the continuing range.
- Plan pot geometry so the river SPR fits your next move (set_up_river_jam for strong lines, size_down_to_see_river for draws).

## Fast Heuristics
- Turn A on K72r gives range lift; go big to pressure Kx and draws.
- Turn brick needs smaller bets or checks to avoid bloating spots where your edge fades.

## Quick Numbers
- Typical turn bet families: big (~70%+), small (~30-40%), and sizing down to keep SPR low.

## Examples (1–2 lines)
- UTG fires bet_big_turn on A two-tone after a small flop bet to push Kx and draw combos off their path.
