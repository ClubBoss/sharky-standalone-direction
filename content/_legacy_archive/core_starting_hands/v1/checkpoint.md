# Checkpoint

## What You Should Know
- Early seats (UTG/MP) must stay tight, while CO/BTN widen because steal equity rises with fewer players.
- Open sizes stay around 2.0-2.5 bb to keep steal ranges wide and deny profitable 3-bets.
- Value 3-bet KK+/AK, sprinkle blocker bluffs (A5s/A4s) in late positions, and respect stack depth for connectors.
- SB should race or fold, and BB defends wider versus small opens but tightens against 3-4 bb raises.
