# Cheatsheet

## Key Rules
- Open tight from UTG/MP and widen on CO/BTN; SB should raise-or-fold while BB mixes wider calls versus small opens.
- Stick to 2.0-2.5 bb opens so steals stay cheap and 3-bet pressure stays meaningful.
- Value 3-bet KK+/AK, and mix in blocker bluffs (A5s, A4s) mainly in late-position battles.
- Let stack depth govern small pairs and suited connectors: open them from CO/BTN when deep, avoid calling OOP versus large 3-bets.

## Fast Heuristics
- UTG opens ~2.2 bb; CO/BTN widen when action folds.
- SB raise-or-fold keeps the pot alive while giving BB a chance to defend.

## Quick Numbers
- Open size: 2.0-2.5 bb online.
- 3-bet value: KK+, AK.

## Examples (1–2 lines)
- UTG opens 2.2 bb, CO 3-bets A5s as a blocker bluff, UTG 4-bets KK, CO folds.
