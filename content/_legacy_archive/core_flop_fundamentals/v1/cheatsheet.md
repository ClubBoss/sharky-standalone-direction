# Cheatsheet

## Key Rules
- Dry board + range edge → small c-bets (25-33%) to charge floats and keep pots manageable.
- Wet/two-tone textures → big c-bets (60-100%) or selective checks; only defend with real equity or blockers.
- Donk-bet or probe-turn when the opponent's range gains nut advantage or when you check back and plan to reload.

## Fast Heuristics
- K72 rainbow: small, frequent bets and potential delayed c-bets afterward.
- T98 two-tone: upsize or target selective checks/raises to keep draws honest.
- Monotone/paired: mix check-backs and keep a close eye on turn changes before double-barreling.

## Quick Numbers
- Small c-bet ≈ 25-33% pot.
- Big c-bet ≈ 60-100% pot.

## Examples (1–2 lines)
- UTG c-bets small on K72r, then reloads after the turn stays dry; on T98tt, he upsizes to deny equity.
