# Cheatsheet

## Key Rules
- Polarize on blanks when you hold nut advantage: mix bet_big_river or overbets with strong values and matching bluffs.
- Thin value in position with top or second pairs that beat worse hands but still face wide calling ranges.
- Bluff with blockers that deny strong calls and do not unblock folds; otherwise, check back.
- Bluffcatch only when price is fair and the villain is polarized; otherwise, fold.

## Fast Heuristics
- Blanks with nut advantage → large bets or overbets.
- Wide callers → bet_small_river with thin values.
- Check when hands have little showdown value or bluff risk.

## Quick Numbers
- Big river bets range from 100% to overbet (>100%) depending on polarization.
- Thin values often live around 30-40% to keep worse hands interested.

## Examples (1–2 lines)
- Polarize with top set/air on a blank river and size big; if a bluffcatch scenario arises with mid pair vs polarized villain, call if price is good.
