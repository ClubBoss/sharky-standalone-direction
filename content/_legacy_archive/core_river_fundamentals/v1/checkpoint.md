# Checkpoint

## What You Should Know
- The river decides equity: choose between polarization, thin value, or bluffcatch based on blockers and sizing.
- Bettor_shows_first after a called river bet; if the river checks through, first_active_left_of_btn_shows.
- Bluffcatch only when price is good and villain is polarized; otherwise, fold or check to preserve chips.
