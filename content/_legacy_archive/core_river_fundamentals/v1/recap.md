# Recap

## Key Ideas
- The river locks equities, so choose between polarization, thin value, and bluffcatch based on blockers, sizing, and the showdown plan.
- Polarize on blanks when you own nut advantage, thin value in position against wide callers, and bluffcatch only with strong blockers.

## Common Mistakes
- Betting medium strength too large, which folds out better hands and dodges calls from worse.
- Bluffing without blockers or folding to every river threat without reading polarization.

## Quick Checks
- When should you thin value instead of polarizing?
- What makes a good bluffcatch candidate on the river?

## Mini-Exercise (1–2 lines)
- Polarize on a missed river draw with blockers; note how blocker presence and showdowns influence size.
