## Range Advantage Basics (SRP)

- BTN opens a tighter range with more high-card combinations, so ace-high boards usually favor button equity.
- BB defends with more combos of connected cards and suited connectors; low connected flops often reward his range.
- Understanding which surface favors which side helps you weigh bluff frequency and protect the nut advantage.

Keep tracking board texture and where the range advantage shifts to keep pressure balanced.
