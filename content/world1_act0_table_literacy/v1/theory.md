# Act 0 Table Literacy

- Start with one anchor: Button is your north star.
- Small Blind is left of Button. Big Blind is next.
- Do not rush actions until seats feel clear.
- A clean seat read saves chips before strategy starts.
- Preflop starts left of the Big Blind.
- After the flop, action starts with the Small Blind, or the first live seat left of Button.
- Button acts last after the flop.
- At showdown, the pot goes to the best five-card hand.
- Use one short ladder: High Card (no pair) < Pair < Two Pair < Trips < Straight < Flush < Full House < Quads < Straight Flush.
- A higher pair beats a lower pair: a pair of Aces beats a pair of Tens.
- The board can play. If the board already makes the best five cards, both players can tie with the same hand.
- Win condition: spot the right seat fast and stay calm.
