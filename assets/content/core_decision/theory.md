# The Golden Rule of Poker

You now know **Outs** (Equity) and **Pot Odds** (Price).
Now you combine them.

* **Step 1:** Count your Outs. (e.g., Flush Draw = 9 outs  36% by River).
* **Step 2:** Calculate Pot Odds. (e.g., Call $50 to win $150 = 25% price).
* **Step 3:** Compare.

**Sharky's Law:**
If **Equity > Price**, you **CALL**. (Buying a dollar for 80 cents).
If **Equity < Price**, you **FOLD**. (Buying a dollar for $1.20).
