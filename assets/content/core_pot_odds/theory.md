# Do I have the right price?

Poker is not about winning every hand. It's about making profitable decisions.
**Pot Odds** tell you if the "Price" of the call is worth the potential "Reward".

### The Formula
$$\text{Odds} = \frac{\text{Amount to Call}}{\text{Total Pot After Call}}$$

### Example
* Pot is **$100**.
* Opponent bets **$50**.
* Total Pot = $150.
* You must call **$50**.
* Total Pool after your call = $100 (start) + $50 (bet) + $50 (call) = **$200**.
* Your Price: $50 / $200 = **25%**.

**Sharky's Rule:** If your chance of winning (Equity) is higher than your Price (Pot Odds), you CALL.
