# Welcome to the Factory
🦈 Sharky: Hi! I'm Sharky. I might look cute, but I'm here to turn you into a poker shark. Ready to hunt?

🎲 [[Skill]] vs. [[Luck]]
New players think poker is like a slot machine. They are wrong.

Short Term: Anyone can win one hand with lucky cards.

Long Term: Over 1,000 hands, [[Skill]] always wins.

This upswing/loss swing is called [[Variance]], so keep your head.

🏠 The House vs. You
In Roulette, the Casino (The House) plays against you. The odds are rigged. In Poker, you play against other people. The Casino just rents the table (takes a "[[Rake]]"). If you are smarter than the other players, you profit.

🦈 Sharky: My job is to make you the smartest player at the table.

🛡️ The Golden Rule
Before we start, promise me one thing: Never bet money you cannot afford to lose. treat your poker money [[Bankroll]] like a business investment, not lottery tickets.
