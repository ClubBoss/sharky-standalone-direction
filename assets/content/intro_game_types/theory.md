# It's Not Just Hold'em

* **No-Limit Hold'em (NLHE):** The Cadillac of poker. 2 cards. Bet as much as you want.
* **Pot-Limit Omaha (PLO):** 4 cards. Crazy action. You can only bet the size of the pot.
* **Tournaments (MTT):** You buy in once, play until one winner remains.
* **Cash Games:** You play with real money value chips. Come and go anytime.
