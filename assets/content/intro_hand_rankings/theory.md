# Hand Rankings
🦈 Sharky: In Poker, he who holds the best cards wins the showdown. Memorize this hierarchy!

👑 [[Royal Flush]]
The King of hands. A-K-Q-J-10, all same suit. Unbeatable.

⚡ [[Straight Flush]]
Five cards in a row, same suit.

4️⃣ [[Quads]]
Four cards of the same rank. Crushes [[Full House]]s.

🏠 [[Full House]]
Three of a kind + A [[Pair]]. "The Boat".

🌊 [[Flush]]
Five cards of the same suit, any order. Sharky Tip: [[Flush]] beats [[Straight]]!

🪜 [[Straight]]
Five cards in a row, different suits.

3️⃣ [[Set]] / [[Trips]]
Three cards of same rank. Also called "[[Set]]" or "[[Trips]]".

2️⃣ [[Two Pair]] & [[Pair]]
Standard hands. [[Two Pair]] beats [[Pair]].

🃏 [[High Card]]
If nobody has anything at [[Showdown]], the highest card wins.

🧠 Quick Terms
[[Pocket Pair]]: When your two hole cards share the same rank (e.g., 7-7 or A-A).
[[Offsuit]]: Two cards from different suits (a Heart and a Spade). They cannot make a [[Flush]] together.
[[Suited]]: Two cards of the same suit. They have a higher chance of making a [[Flush]].
[[Stack]]: Every chip you hold at the table. Protect it like a treasure chest.
[[Big Slick]]: A nickname for Ace-King (AK). One of the most famous hands.
[[Kicker]]: The unmatched card in your hand. If two players have the same [[Pair]], the higher [[Kicker]] wins.
