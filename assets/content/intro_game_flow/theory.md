# From Preflop to River

Poker is a story told in four chapters (Streets):

1. **Preflop:** You get 2 cards. You bet or fold.
2. **The Flop:** 3 community cards are revealed. This changes everything.
3. **The Turn:** 1 more card. The pot gets bigger.
4. **The River:** The final card. The truth comes out.

**Sharky's Rule:** Don't pay to see the River if you know you're beaten on the Turn.
