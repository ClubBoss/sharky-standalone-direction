# Don't Guess the Exact Hand

Beginners put opponents on ONE hand ("He has Aces!").
Pros put opponents on a **RANGE** of hands ("He has strong pairs or Ace-King").

A **Range** is the collection of all hands a player might hold in a specific situation.

* **Tight Range:** Only the best hands (AA, KK, QQ, AK).
* **Wide Range:** Almost anything (Pairs, Suited Connectors, Trash).
