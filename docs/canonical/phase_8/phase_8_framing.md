# Phase 8 Framing - SSOT

## 1) Intent
Phase 8 exists to gather evidence, metrics, and stakeholder alignment for what comes after the content expansion baseline; it frames possible levers (visual, UX, meta-loop) without choosing one.

## 2) Candidate directions (non-exhaustive)
- Personalization tuning — refine recommendations, pacing, or tone without altering core flow.  
- Economy/progression balance — revisit rewards, chips, or milestones linked to first sessions.  
- Visual & UX polish — deepen the Visual Lift contract into full polish, respecting Phase 6 guardrails.  
- Meta-loop resilience — explore retention loops, boredom prevention, or repeat play that honor the Golden Hour SSOT.

## 3) Non-goals
- Not committing to any single direction or solution.  
- Not altering the First 5 Minutes flow, intro content, or telemetry baselines.  
- Not redesigning core session mechanics, Modern Table visuals, or new content schemas.

## 4) Entry conditions
- Phase 7 completion documented (`phase_7_completion_and_transition.md`).  
- Intro path, guard tests, and First 5 Minutes telemetry fully locked.  
- Content expansion tooling (`docs/master_plan_7/content_expansion_execution_plan.md`) validated.

## 5) Exit signal (high-level)
- A clear decision brief (Phase 8 SSOT) defines which of the candidate directions will proceed, backed by data/ops signals and stakeholder sign-off.  

## 6) Decision guard
- No prototype or implementation proceeds until a dedicated Phase 8 SSOT is written referencing this framing document and the evidence that motivates choosing a direction.
