1. `75-79`: strong shaping product, but still clearly pre-launch in route truth

## What This Does And Does Not Mean

### What it means

1. `Volume I` is now credible as a real launch-shaped route.
2. The active bottleneck is no longer "is there enough route?"
3. The next macro move should focus on launch polish, launch packaging, and
   honest monetization implementation.

### What it does not mean

1. It does not mean true release readiness is `81 / 100`.
2. It does not mean store, entitlement, ops, submission, or legal/compliance
   truth is solved.
3. It does not justify opening `W13+` early just because the first twelve
   worlds are stronger now.
