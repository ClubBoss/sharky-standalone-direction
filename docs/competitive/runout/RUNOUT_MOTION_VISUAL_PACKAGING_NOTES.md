## Video / Motion Assets

- A paywall video asset is present, indicating motion is used in commercial packaging.

## Premium Background / Visual Asset Categories

- Branded table background.
- Onboarding welcome/center/top-gradient backgrounds.
- Glass panel backgrounds in multiple colors/sizes.
- Paywall and discount backgrounds.
- Skill/library/mastery tour backgrounds.
- Progress/mistake/winner chart visuals.
- Poker concept visuals for preflop, postflop, positions, theory, and hand reading.

## Chart / Analytics Motifs

- Mistakes, winners, progress, frequencies, trend, report, analytics icons.
- These motifs make the app look data-rich and serious.
- Risk: charts can feel like proof even when the first hand has not taught anything yet.

## Animation / Product Feel Ideas

- Use micro-motion to acknowledge answer -> signal -> feedback.
- Keep feedback motion local to the table object being explained.
- Use progress/chart visuals only after the learner has generated real task data.
- Use premium backgrounds sparingly in Sharky; table readability wins over decorative gloss.

## Sharky-Safe Adaptation Principles

- Borrow the principle of poker-specific premium packaging, not visual style.
- Make the table the hero visual asset.
- Animate causal proof, not generic celebration.
- Prefer compact, deterministic signal badges over decorative panels.
- Do not copy Runout video, backgrounds, glass assets, typography, icons, or chart layouts.
