# Runout Progress And Retention Notes

## Evidence Used

- APK assets: daily-session tour image, skill-library tour, skill-mastery path, progress chart background, mistake and winner chart lines, streak top background, tracker tab icon.
- Bundle taxonomy: daily/session, skill/mastery, analytics/report/chart, tracker/history/session summary, progress, mistakes.
- XAPK permissions: notifications, scheduled alarms, boot/wake/background service support.

## Daily Sessions

- Runout appears to emphasize a recurring daily trainer/session habit.
- Session-length and trainer-readiness token families suggest the daily loop is configurable or personalized.
- Notification/alarm permissions suggest retention nudges are part of the product infrastructure.

## Skill Library / Mastery Path

- Skill library and mastery path assets point to a visible skill taxonomy.
- Tier/rating/profile tokens imply learner identity and performance classification.
- This creates a strong "I am improving over time" frame.

## Analytics / Tracker Surfaces

- Analytics, tracker, hand, session history, and report tokens imply a serious measurement layer.
- Hand recording/replayer/session history clues suggest the app connects study to real play logs.
- Chart assets suggest progress is visualized, not just counted.

## Streak / Progress / XP-Like Clues

- Streak image assets exist even though raw keyword count for "streak" was low.
- Progress, chart, daily session, skill mastery, and profile/rating signals together imply a retention stack.
- The product likely leans on visible continuity: daily work, skill growth, mistakes, and stats.

## How Sharky Can Build A Better Deterministic Version

- First-value receipt should say what was learned from the table, not just that a rep was completed.
- Progress should store and show deterministic signals: table read, action word, position, pot/to-call, board read.
- Review should repair specific missed signals instead of generic mistakes.
- Home/Today should start with one recommended repair or transfer, not a dashboard.
- Skill growth should be traceable to exact Act0 task/result metadata.

## Sharky-Safe Retention Principle

Retention should come from "I can see myself getting better at table reads" rather than pressure, streak anxiety, or broad analytics density.
