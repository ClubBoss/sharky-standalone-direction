control plane.

## What Is Most Likely To Be Overlooked

There is no longer one dominant missing content-system packet.

The remaining risks are mostly execution-side:

1. world density and quality at production time
2. keeping future copy additions aligned with the calmer tone
3. not reopening heavy governance drift after the control plane is already clear

## Recommended Next Step

Stop creating new governance packets by default.

Return to product/content production unless a new real ambiguity appears.

## Bottom Line

The project now has effectively complete coverage lock at the
concept-system level.

What remains is not a giant forgotten poker block.

What remains is making sure the course is:

1. transferable to real play
2. scalable through tiers and resurfacing
3. governed by a clean active authoring and promotion loop

That control plane is now substantially in place.
The next frontier is using it well, not expanding it further.
