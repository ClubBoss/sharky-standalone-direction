## 8. Task Arc Assessment

The arc is coherent:

1. Visible ace reduction introduces card-removal combo density.
2. Visible king transfer repeats the same idea on a different rank.
3. Paired-board texture applies visible-card reduction to board texture.
4. Visible-card density transfer checks generalization across boards/ranks.

This is enough for an internal mini-world, not a public course claim.

and later correct evidence becomes a non-causal proof-compatible signal.
