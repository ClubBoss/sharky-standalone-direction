## 7. W8 Concept Family Decision

- `w8_draw_improvement_potential`

Decision: teach beginner-readable recognition of when a draw has meaningful
improvement potential without raw equity math, route admission, or public
claims.

## 8. W8 Task List And Learning Purpose

1. `flush_draw_recognition_intro`: suited future cards can improve a flush
   draw.
2. `open_ended_straight_draw_intro`: cards on either end can complete an
   open-ended straight draw.
3. `gutshot_vs_open_ended_comparison_lite`: an open-ended draw has more clear
   improvement paths than a one-gap draw.
4. `draw_improvement_potential_transfer_check`: transfer the improvement idea
   across draw vs no-clear-draw spots.
