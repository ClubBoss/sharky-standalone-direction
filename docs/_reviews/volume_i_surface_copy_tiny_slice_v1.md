## 3. Copy principles

1. Show honest depth, not fake breadth.
2. Describe a route-backed current path before a planned horizon.
3. `Planned` means non-clickable and coming later; it is not a locked product.
4. A frontier is a future landmark, never an unlock promise.
5. Use short, beginner-safe words and introduce no new poker jargon here.
6. Do not copy a library, category, chapter-count, or academy framing from
   Runout or another product.
7. Preserve one dominant continuation. Home dispatches to it; Learn owns the
   forward-path explanation; Profile only mirrors concise progress context.

- `Complete W1-W12`

  upgrade wording.
