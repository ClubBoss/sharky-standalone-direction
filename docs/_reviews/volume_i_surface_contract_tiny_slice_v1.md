## 3. Surface purpose

The smallest truthful surface should make Sharky feel like a route rather than
a small proof app: it shows the present route-backed depth through W10, the
honest W11-W12 foundation horizon, and the later W13+ frontier. It earns trust
by making no false completion, commercial-access, AI, or specialization claim.

It remains a compact route horizon with one dominant current continuation, not
a curriculum dashboard, mastery map, academy library, or commerce gateway.

## 5. Copy contract

Safe examples:

- Current: `Continue the current campaign through W10.`
- Planned: `W11-W12 are planned foundation chapters, coming later.`
- Later: `Later strategic depth follows the shared foundation.`
- More depth: `A clear next horizon, without pretending it is playable today.`
- Continue current: `Continue current route.`

Forbidden examples:

- `Finish Volume I now.`
- `Unlock W13.`
- `Premium specialization available.`
- Any AI/adaptive/leak-resurfacing claim.
- `Complete Cash/MTT program available.`
- `Mastery path live.`

## 6. Status badge contract

Allowed badges are `Available`, `Current campaign`, `Planned`, `Later`, and
`Advanced frontier`. A badge describes status only; it is not a value,
completion, or entitlement marker.

Forbidden badges are `Premium`, `Unlocked`, `Complete`, `AI`, `Mastered`, and
`Specialization ready`.

## 7. Interaction contract

- W1-W10 may link only to their already-owned current route or campaign.
- W11-W12 must remain non-clickable planned markers.
- W13+ must remain non-clickable frontier markers.
- Do not add commerce, an entitlement gateway, a W12-to-W13 handoff, or a
  mastery dashboard.

## 8. Proof upgrade requirements

| Claim to upgrade | Minimum evidence before surface language changes |
| --- | --- |
| W11-W12 from planned to campaign/current | Active shelves, canonical pack/session registration, learner entry, focused route proof, and an admitted readiness contract. |
| W13+ from later to playable | Owned authored content, canonical route ownership, learner-visible entry, focused route proof, and status-contract revision. |
| Commercial access | A separate explicit entitlement, access, and value-before-paywall contract; route reach alone is insufficient. |
| Mastery, AI, or leak-resurfacing | Owned learner behavior and proof for that named mechanism; classifier metadata or planned architecture is insufficient. |
| Specialization | Explicit specialization policy, route ownership, content proof, and readiness proof for the named path. |
