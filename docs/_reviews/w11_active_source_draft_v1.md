## 3. W11 content substance

The W11 objective is a calm transfer loop: choose one observable focus, notice
a visible table signal, apply an earlier concept, make a clear decision, and
record one factual review observation. `w11.s01` has six planned reps around a
weak-draw price focus. Position, player tendency, board texture, and price lead
to a justified continue or fold, then a short recheck and review close.

The source basis is the W11 Real Play Transfer/capstone curriculum intent, the
Content System micro-session contract, and the Unified Learning Architecture
transfer requirement. It reuses W2 position, W4 price and bet purpose, W5 board
and draws, W6 range buckets, and W9-W10 visible opponent/table cues. One
session with no drill payload is not broad content expansion.
