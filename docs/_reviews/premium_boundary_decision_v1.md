# Premium Boundary Decision v1

## 1. Verdict

`boundary_decision_ready_no_code`

## 2. Current truth

- W1-W6 has accepted first-value route proof; W7-W10 is the current campaign
  continuation.
- W11-W12 is planned foundation only. W13-W24 is later strategic frontier and
  W25-W36 is deferred advanced specialization.
- W1-W12 describes the intended shared Volume I foundation, not a currently
  completable route. W13+ is not unlockable.
- The canonical Act0 route has no hard paywall, pricing, purchase, restore,
  trial-start, subscription, entitlement change, or world gate.
- A boundary-neutral post-value preview may explain future depth but does not
  transact, name access, or change a learner's route.
- No AI/adaptive, mastery/leak, or specialization behavior is owned for a
  learner-facing commercial claim.

## 4. Recommended boundary

Adopt **W1-W4 free, W5+ future paid depth** now as the strategic contract only.

It does not authorize a W5 gate, a paywall, price, trial, purchase, upgrade
CTA, or entitlement change. W5+ may be described only as future depth after
the free foundation until content, route, commerce, and value proof all close.
The W1-W6 first-value proof does not convert W5/W6 into current paid access.

## 6. Safe copy / forbidden copy

Safe future, non-commercial examples:

- `The current route continues through W10.`
- `More depth is planned after the free foundation.`
- `W11-W12 are planned foundation chapters, coming later.`
- `No purchase is needed for the current route.`

Forbidden active copy includes `Unlock W13`, `Upgrade to continue`, `Premium
specialization`, `Start your trial`, `Subscribe`, `Purchase`, `Restore
purchase`, `Paid depth available now`, `Full Cash/MTT tracks`, `AI fixes your
leaks`, `Mastery path live`, `Become pro`, `Complete Volume I now`, and any
scarcity/countdown pressure. The Value-Before-Paywall Copy Guard must continue
blocking these claims on active Act0 copy owners.

soft-preview vocabulary without introducing commerce. It does not make W5+
paid today, make W11-W12 playable, make W13 unlockable, create specialization,
or prove AI, mastery, leak, Cash, or MTT behavior.
