## 5. Sequence audit

The core order is sound: rules -> hand discipline -> position -> preflop ->
bet purpose/price -> board/draws -> ranges -> stack context -> format pressure
-> adjustment -> transfer -> variance. It prevents range theory before table
literacy and math before price intuition.

Bounded jump risks:

1. **W6 to W7:** ranges can become abstract unless W6 table signals are used
   as the first range filters. Keep W7 bucket-first.
2. **W8 to W9:** tournament pressure follows baseline skill, but ICM must stay
   lightweight in W9 and formal in W26.
3. **W12 to W13:** this is the largest frontier jump: variance literacy does
   not itself prove postflop range-construction readiness. Keep W13 locked
   until Volume I route truth is reconciled.
4. **W14-W16:** sizing, c-bet responses, and river choices must retain the
   `purpose -> board -> range -> commitment` order rather than becoming a
   vocabulary dump.
5. **W21-W29:** review, stats, and study terms require a real evidence source;
   do not imply database or leak tooling exists now.

## 6. Runout benchmark comparison

Runout is visibly broader through a large skills/library presentation, early
preflop and response families, visual explainer categories, and commercial
packaging. Sharky intentionally differs with a finite guided route and a
deterministic `choice -> table signal -> why -> repair/recheck` loop.

The benchmark confirms Sharky must retain c-bet/response, sizing, multiway,
blocker, SPR, and tournament coverage. It does not justify copying chapter
rows, categories, assets, text, layouts, progress theatre, or paywall timing.
Visual explainers are valuable later for ranges, sizing, blockers, equity, and
street progression—but only when they clarify a live decision signal.

## 7. Sharky differentiators to preserve

- Table-first decision ladder and one signal at a time.
- Deterministic repair loop, not generic progress theatre.
- Early mental survival through decision-quality-versus-outcome framing.
- Transfer from a rep to real-table observation and action.
- Finite core path, with mastery tiers and leak resurfacing only when owned.
- Value before any paywall; W1-W4 free and W5+ future paid depth remain
  boundaries, not current commerce behavior.
- Beginner cognitive safety: no solver-first language, charts, or fake AI.
