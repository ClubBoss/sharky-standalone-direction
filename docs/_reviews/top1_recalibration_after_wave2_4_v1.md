## 1. Why This Wave Exists

Wave 2.4 closed a controlled beta handoff packet. That means Sharky is ready
for narrow external review of one beginner-safe proof loop; it does not mean
Sharky is ready for a public premium top-1 launch.

This recalibration preserves the proof-loop discipline from Waves 1-2.4 while
raising the target: Sharky now routes toward a published premium table-first
poker learning product that can credibly compete with best-in-class alternatives
on first-loop clarity, learning effect, premium feel, emotional payoff, retention,
claim safety, and value timing.

## 3. New TOP1 Target Definition

Sharky should become a premium table-first poker learning product that wins by
making a beginner feel, quickly and honestly:

`I saw the table clue -> I made a better decision -> I repaired one mistake -> I have local proof.`

The target product wins through:

- first-start clarity;
- beginner-safe table learning;
- table-signal feedback;
- deterministic repair;
- local proof;
- satisfying payoff;
- premium mobile feel;
- compact Sharky coach / product soul;
- earned rewards;
- habit rhythm;
- honest Review and Profile proof;
- value-after-proof monetization;
- store-safe claims.

## 5. Runout / Competitor Wedge

Where Sharky can beat Runout:

- faster first-loop proof;
- beginner-safe table-signal explanation;
- deterministic repair instead of vague improvement promise;
- trust-safe onboarding before any paid ask;
- emotional payoff from local proof, not broad analytics theater.

Where Runout is stronger for now:

- commercial packaging;
- subscription and paywall infrastructure;
- motion/media polish;
- analytics/report breadth;
- feature breadth and mature app scaffolding.

What Sharky must not copy:

- solver/GTO posture;
- proprietary visuals, layouts, or assets;
- report/chart theater before proof;
- paywall ceremony before value;
- feature soup that buries the table-learning loop.

What Sharky must prove before public release:

- premium table/session feel;
- repeatable first-week value;
- honest Review/Profile coaching depth;
- compact Sharky emotional identity;
- value-after-proof premium packaging;
- store-safe claims and screenshots.

## 6. Operating Methodology For Future Waves

Every future top-1 wave should state:

- TOP1 row target: the exact matrix row or rows being moved.
- 10/10 acceptance bar: what would make the row public-product credible.
- Anti-drift boundary: what the wave must not reopen or imitate.
- Commercial proof signal: why this matters for a premium top-1 product.
- Evidence type: docs, tests, screenshot packet, beta notes, telemetry, or
  external visual/UX challenge.
- Expected score movement: conservative before/after band.
- Rollback / dormant hypothesis rule: if validation does not move the row, the
  idea goes dormant instead of expanding scope.
